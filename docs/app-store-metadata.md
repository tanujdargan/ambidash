## Description
AmbiDash is a life dashboard for ambitious people who juggle too many goals and forget half of them.

It doesn't gamify your habits. It doesn't nag you with streaks. It watches — quietly — and asks better questions than you'd ask yourself.

**What it does:**
- Understands you through standardized assessments (ADHD-adapted, values, work style)
- Connects to Apple Health, Calendar, and your notes to see how you're actually living
- Generates a daily action plan balanced across all your life pillars
- Tracks your vitals: Body, Mind, Craft, People, Wealth, Adventure
- Provides an AI mentor that speaks in letters, not chat bubbles
- Shows honest trends — no badges, no points, just data

**How it's different:**
- Anti-gamification: trends and identity statements replace streaks and XP
- Diminishing AI: the mentor starts hands-on and fades as you build self-reliance
- Privacy-first: all data stays on your device. Health data never leaves your phone.
- Six life pillars, not five categories someone else picked

**For people who:**
- Have 10 goals and act on 3
- Know what they should do but can't maintain all of it
- Want accountability that doesn't feel like a game

Free with basic features. Premium unlocks unlimited AI mentor, real-time pattern spotting, and monthly deep dives.
